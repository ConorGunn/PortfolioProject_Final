About

I am an Irish college student studying Creative Digital Media at Institute of Technology Blanchardstown (ITB). I am interested in areas such as writing, acting, directing, editing, web design and graphic design. From a very young age I have had an interest in the creative industry. At the age of seven I entered a short film into an Irish film festival meant for film makers much older than me. From the years of 2009 to 2014 I attended the Irish Film Academy where I learned a variety of techniques and got a realistic experince to the film world.
